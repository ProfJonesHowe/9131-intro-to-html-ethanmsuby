What Is Science-Based Lifting?

Science-based lifting is an approach to strength training that uses evidence from exercise science and physiology to optimize performance, recovery, and muscle growth. It involves structured programming, progressive overload, and proper technique backed by research.

Fun Facts About Lifting

Did you know that compound lifts like squats and deadlifts engage multiple muscle groups and can boost testosterone levels? Or that your muscles grow not during the workout, but while you rest and recover afterward?
